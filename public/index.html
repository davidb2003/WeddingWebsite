About Kalkidan

Born in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia Kalkidan migrated to the United States with her parents at the age of five. She was raised in Fresno, CA and Nashville TN. She graduated from the University of Alabama in Huntsville with a Bachelor's of Science Nursing degree. She currently resides in Kansas City, KS and works at Childrens Mercy Hospital.

About David

Born in the sunny slopes of San Bernadinio. This California native moved to Huntsville, AL where he earned a degree in Computer Engineering from the University of Alabama in Huntsville. He works as a Software Engineer at Cerner Corporation in Kansis City. In his spare time, David enjoys programming, listening to public radio, and reading good science fiction.
