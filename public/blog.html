Temecula

Temecula is located in the southwestern region of the Riverside County of California. Temecula is bordered by the City of Murrieta to the north and the Pechanga Indian Reservation and San Diego County to the south. The City of Temecula forms the southwestern anchor of the Inland Empire region.

The city is prominently a tourist destination, with the Temecula Valley Wine Country, Old Town Temecula, and Temecula Valley Polo Club. Two of the major annual festivals includes Temecula Valley Balloon & Wine Festival, and the Temecula Valley International Film Festival. Activities in the area included but are not limited to hot air balloon rides, wine tours and golfing on championship golf courses just to name a few.

How to get there:

There are four airports in the area with the average drive time to Wilson Creek Winery: LAX (99.4miles average drive 2.5 hours drive), John Wayne Orange County (72.4miles average 1.5 hours drive), Ontario (59.3miles, average drive 1 hour) and San Diego (68.5 miles, average drive 1.25 hours).

Travel advice:

To those that are new to the Southern California area. Please give yourself ample time to drive because there is ALWAYS traffic anywhere/everywhere that you go! Also everything including car rental and gas is more expensive in California!
